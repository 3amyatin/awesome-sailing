# Croatia

Croatia is one of Europe's busiest charter and flotilla destinations, so this page should prioritize practical trip-planning, marina, weather, and paperwork resources.

## Overview
- Use this page to move quickly from official references into marina, weather, and arrival planning.
- The links here focus on practical cruising decisions for the Adriatic rather than broad travel content.

## Official information
- [eNautika](https://enautika.pomorstvo.hr/) - Official Croatian nautical portal for registrations, notices, and trip preparation.
- [Croatia.hr Nautika PDF](https://www.htz.hr/sites/default/files/2023-09/Nautika%20-%20ENG.pdf) - Official tourism guide with a nautical focus and practical background information.

## Ports and marinas
- [ACI Marinas](https://aci-marinas.com/) - Major marina network with berth, service, and destination information.
- [eNavtika Adriatic Guide](https://enavtika.si/en/adriatic-guide) - Practical Adriatic POI and route-planning reference.

## Weather
- [Meteo.hr Adriatic forecast](https://meteo.hr/prognoze_e.php?param=jadran&section=prognoze_spec) - Official Adriatic marine forecast page.
- [DHMZ English text forecast](https://meteo.hr/prognoze_e.php?section=prognoze_specp&param=jadran&el=jadran_e) - Direct English marine text forecast for the Adriatic. 🇭🇷

## Rules and paperwork
- [eNautika](https://enautika.pomorstvo.hr/) - Start here for Croatian nautical administration and trip paperwork.
- [Croatia.hr nautical fee leaflet](https://croatia.hr/cmsmedia/Documents/Nautical/TP_za_nauticare/Novi_letak/HTZ-2022_letak_nautika-pristojba_QR%20code_EN.pdf) - Practical official leaflet for the nautical fee and related visitor guidance.
- [Plovput Navtex](https://www.plovput.hr/en/radio-service/navtex-system) - Official navigational message source for the Croatian coast. 🇭🇷

## Charter and local operators
- [ACI Marinas](https://aci-marinas.com/) - Good first stop for marina-based charter and services across the coast.

## Local apps and maps
- [Meteo.hr Adriatic forecast](https://meteo.hr/prognoze_e.php?param=jadran&section=prognoze_spec) - Weather reference that pairs well with route and anchorage planning.
- [eNavtika Adriatic Guide](https://enavtika.si/en/adriatic-guide) - Useful route-planning reference with Adriatic POIs and place context.
