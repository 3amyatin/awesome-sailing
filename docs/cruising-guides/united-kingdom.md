# United Kingdom

The United Kingdom is best approached as a weather-first cruising guide: official forecasts, strong national sailing infrastructure, and a Scotland-aware gateway rather than a full coast-by-coast pilot guide.

## Overview
- Use this page as a compact starting point for UK coastal planning before you narrow into Solent, Channel, west-coast, or Scotland detail.
- The strongest references here are official forecast tools plus broad sailing gateways.

## Official information
- [RYA](https://www.rya.org.uk/) - Main UK sailing authority and a useful first stop for local sailing context. 🇬🇧

## Ports and marinas
- [Sail Scotland](https://sailscotland.co.uk/) - Strong public gateway for Scottish sailing areas, marinas, and cruising inspiration. 🇬🇧

## Weather
- [Met Office Shipping Forecast](https://weather.metoffice.gov.uk/specialist-forecasts/coast-and-sea/shipping-forecast) - Official UK shipping forecast entry point. 🇬🇧
- [Met Office Inshore Waters Forecast](https://weather.metoffice.gov.uk/specialist-forecasts/coast-and-sea/inshore-waters-forecast) - Official UK inshore forecast reference. 🇬🇧

## Safety and regulations
- [GOV.UK Safety at sea](https://www.gov.uk/owning-a-boat/safety-at-sea) - Practical UK government safety guidance that is worth checking before coastal passages. 🇬🇧
